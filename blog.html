"Impact of Techononlogy"

Technology, the most powerful and revolutionary word in the 21st century. Although the base of the technology has been laid by different scientists and researchers of early centuries it is growing and developing in exponential rate in today’s data like never before and it will most probably keep on growing in coming future too. We are surrounded the technology in every aspect of our life. And in today’s time when we all are facing the deadliest and catastrophic pandemic it has been important than ever before where all people are forced to be stuck in their home and doing all kind of work remotely form teaching, reading, meetings, banking, shopping, jobs, health care, booking and many more.

Personally, technology has great impact in my life, especially being the student of IT, it is compulsion for me to have the proper tools for studying as well as for preparing my shelf in career path. From, online classes to entertainment purposes, even washing clothes to cooking food, cleaning home, communicating to friends and families the technology plays a vital role for me. I cannot imagine the life without technology. It will lot more difficult and probably unbearable to imagine life without technology.

For someone who love the technology very much it is very much connected to my heart. I would like to introduce my shelf as techy person as I always gets excited about thinking and getting to learn about the new technologies and their benefits. I like to read about the new researches that are being conducted in about the new possibilities for future technologies from spaceflight, artificial intelligence, virtual reality, augmented reality, and so on.
